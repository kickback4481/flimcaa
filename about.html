About Dr. John Monhollon, MD

Integrative Medicine in Sarasota, Florida

Our Medical Director

Dr. John Pylant Monhollon, MD is a board-certified family medicine physician and Medical Director of Florida Integrative Medical Center in Sarasota, Florida. With more than 25 years of clinical experience, Dr. Monhollon is dedicated to providing personalized, patient-centered care that integrates traditional medicine with functional and integrative approaches to health and wellness.

Throughout his career, Dr. Monhollon has focused on helping patients address complex and chronic health concerns through comprehensive evaluation, advanced diagnostics, and individualized care planning. His approach emphasizes understanding the whole person—not simply managing symptoms—by considering lifestyle factors, nutrition, environmental influences, medical history, and overall wellness goals.

Dr. Monhollon has pursued extensive continuing education in integrative medicine, chronic illness, and tick-borne disease management. He completed advanced physician training through the International Lyme and Associated Diseases Society (ILADS) and has subsequently served as a physician mentor for other clinicians seeking advanced education in Lyme disease and related complex chronic conditions. This experience has made him a sought-after resource for patients seeking a more comprehensive evaluation of chronic and difficult-to-resolve health concerns.

Dr. Monhollon earned his medical degree from St. George’s University School of Medicine in 2000. Following his residency in family medicine, he completed an additional year of pathology training to further his understanding of disease processes and diagnostic evaluation. He is affiliated with Sarasota Memorial Hospital and Orlando Health Bayfront Hospital and is a member of the American College for Advancement in Medicine (ACAM).

Dr. Monhollon’s Approach to Patient-Centered Care

At FLIMC, Dr. Monhollon works with patients seeking support for chronic health challenges, immune system concerns, Lyme disease and other tick-borne illnesses, preventive wellness, healthy aging, and overall quality of life. His philosophy of care is built on collaboration, education, and long-term relationships that empower patients to take an active role in their health journey.

Known for his compassionate bedside manner and commitment to individualized care, Dr. Monhollon works closely with each patient to develop practical, personalized strategies designed to support long-term health and wellness.
