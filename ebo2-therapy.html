EBO₂ (Extracorporeal Blood Oxygenation & Ozonation)

EBO₂ (Extracorporeal Blood Oxygenation & Ozonation) is an advanced, physician-guided wellness therapy that combines blood filtration, oxygenation, and medical-grade ozone as part of a personalized integrative care plan. During the procedure, blood is continuously circulated through a specialized extracorporeal system where it passes through a high-performance filter before being enriched with oxygen and precisely administered medical-grade ozone. The blood is then returned to the body in a continuous cycle throughout the session.

This multi-step process is designed to support the body’s natural physiological functions by helping optimize oxygen delivery, encourage healthy circulation, and reduce the burden of unwanted particles that may accumulate in the bloodstream. By combining filtration with oxygenation and ozone, EBO₂ provides a comprehensive approach to supporting overall wellness.

Many patients choose EBO₂ as part of their wellness program to support:

  • Healthy circulation and vascular wellness
  • Oxygen delivery throughout the body
  • Advanced blood filtration
  • Cellular wellness and energy production
  • Healthy immune system function
  • A balanced inflammatory response
  • Healthy detoxification pathways
  • Overall vitality and healthy aging
  • Recovery from everyday physical and environmental stressors

Every EBO₂ session is performed under physician supervision using specialized medical equipment and individualized protocols based on each patient’s health history, wellness goals, and overall care plan. Because every individual is different, your provider will determine whether EBO₂ may be an appropriate addition to your personalized wellness program.
