Extracorporeal Blood Oxygenation and Ozonation.

EBOO (Extracorporeal Blood Oxygenation and Ozonation)

EBOO (Extracorporeal Blood Oxygenation and Ozonation) is one of the most advanced forms of ozone-based blood filtration and oxygenation available in integrative medicine. This sophisticated, dialysis-style wellness therapy continuously circulates blood through a specialized extracorporeal system where it is gently filtered, oxygenated, and exposed to medical-grade ozone before being returned to the body. Unlike traditional ozone therapies that process a limited amount of blood, EBOO is designed to circulate and support a significantly larger blood volume during a single session, providing a more comprehensive, full-body approach to wellness.

As blood moves through the EBOO system, it passes through a highly specialized filtration device that helps remove certain unwanted particles and impurities from circulation while simultaneously increasing oxygen availability and supporting healthy blood flow. Medical-grade ozone is then introduced in carefully controlled amounts, where it interacts with the blood to encourage a variety of beneficial physiological responses. The combination of filtration, oxygenation, and ozonation makes EBOO a unique therapy designed to support the body’s natural ability to maintain balance and optimal function.

EBOO is often chosen by individuals seeking advanced support for overall wellness, healthy aging, immune balance, detoxification pathways, circulation, and cellular vitality. Because oxygen is essential for virtually every cell in the body, improving oxygen delivery may help support healthy energy production, normal cellular metabolism, and overall physiological performance. Many patients also choose EBOO as part of a comprehensive wellness program designed to promote resilience against environmental stressors and support long-term health.

Medical-grade ozone has been studied for its ability to support healthy oxidative balance and encourage the body’s own antioxidant defenses. When administered by trained medical professionals using specialized equipment, ozone may help stimulate normal cellular signaling pathways involved in immune regulation, circulation, and healthy inflammatory balance. These responses are intended to support the body’s natural healing processes and promote overall physiological wellness.

The advanced filtration component of EBOO further distinguishes it from many other ozone therapies. During circulation, the filtration system may assist in reducing the burden of circulating debris, inflammatory byproducts, and other unwanted substances that can accumulate over time from environmental exposures, lifestyle factors, and normal metabolic processes. Supporting these natural detoxification pathways may help optimize the body’s internal environment and contribute to improved overall vitality.

Many individuals incorporate EBOO into their personalized wellness plans to support:

  • Healthy immune system function
  • Natural detoxification processes
  • Cellular oxygen utilization
  • Healthy circulation and blood flow
  • Mitochondrial energy production
  • Healthy inflammatory balance
  • Cardiovascular wellness

  • Physical recovery and exercise performance
  • Overall vitality and healthy aging
